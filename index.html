About Me

Welcome to my creative corner of the web!
I’m Riya, a 3rd-year IT student with a passion for web development and design, where functionality meets aesthetic magic. With a keen eye for detail and an obsession for crafting sleek, responsive, and visually captivating websites, I’ve spent the last few years mastering the art of front-end development. My toolkit includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, React, and Next.js—essential ingredients for building digital experiences that are not only user-friendly but downright unforgettable. As a student who’s constantly absorbing new knowledge, I’m always at the cutting edge, keeping up with the latest trends and evolving my skills. Each project I take on feels like a new adventure—whether it’s creating an elegant portfolio, designing a dynamic web app, or shaping an intricate interface, I strive to blend innovation with practicality. Problem-solving is my playground, and I enjoy bringing creative solutions to every challenge, all while making sure the end result looks as good as it works. When I’m not deep in code, you’ll find me exploring fresh design ideas, contributing to open-source projects, or diving into web development resources to keep my mind sharp. I believe collaboration is key to innovation, and I’m excited to connect with others who share my passion for building exceptional digital experiences. Thanks for stopping by—take a look around my portfolio, and if you’re interested in working together, let’s build something incredible!
